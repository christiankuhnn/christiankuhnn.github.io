Internet Security Training

Welcome to our internet security training course. Here you will learn the basics of staying safe online and protecting your personal and business information.

Lesson 1: Phishing Links

Phishing links are fraudulent links that appear to be legitimate websites. They are used by hackers to trick you into giving them your personal and business information. To avoid falling victim to phishing links, follow these simple steps:

  1. Never click on links in unsolicited emails or text messages.
  2. Verify the URL of the website before entering any personal or business information.
  3. Use anti-phishing software to protect against known phishing attacks.

Lesson 2: Safe Passwords

Safe passwords are an essential part of internet security. To create a safe password, follow these guidelines:

  • Use a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Use a unique password for each account.
  • Change your passwords regularly.

Lesson 3: Data Protection

Protecting your personal and business data is crucial to internet security. Follow these tips to keep your data safe:

  • Use secure connections when transmitting sensitive information.
  • Encrypt your data to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regularly backup your data to protect against data loss.
