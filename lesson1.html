Lesson 1: Public Wi-Fi

Public Wi-Fi networks are a convenient way to access the internet when you're out and about, but they also come with security risks. Hackers can exploit the vulnerabilities of public Wi-Fi networks to steal sensitive information, such as passwords, credit card details, and personal data. In this article, we'll discuss some of the common security issues associated with public Wi-Fi networks and offer tips on how to stay safe.

Here are some Security Issues of Public Wi-Fi:

  • Man-in-the-Middle (MITM) Attacks
  • Malware Infections
  • Rogue Access Points
  • Unencrypted Data

In conclusion, public Wi-Fi networks can be a security risk, but by taking the necessary precautions, you can protect your personal information and stay safe online. Remember to use encryption technologies, verify the legitimacy of Wi-Fi hotspots, and avoid accessing sensitive information when connected to public Wi-Fi. Stay safe and secure!
