Why are Safe Passwords Important?

Using a weak password can make it easy for hackers to gain access to your accounts, putting your personal information at risk. By using a strong and unique password for each of your accounts, you can greatly reduce the chances of your accounts being compromised.

Tips for Creating Strong Passwords

  • Use a mix of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Don't use easily guessable information like your name or birthdate
  • Use a passphrase instead of a password
  • Consider using a password manager to generate and store unique passwords for each account

Practice Activity

Take a moment to think about your current passwords. Do they follow the tips listed above? If not, it's time to update them. Use a password manager to generate new, unique passwords for each of your accounts.
