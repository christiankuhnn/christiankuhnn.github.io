What are Phishing Links?

Phishing links are fake links that are designed to look like legitimate links to trick you into giving away personal information, like your username and password.

How to Spot Phishing Links

  • Check the URL - hover over the link to see if the URL matches the site it claims to be
  • Check for typos and other errors in the text
  • Be wary of urgent or threatening language, as this is a common tactic used by scammers
  • Don't click on links in unsolicited emails or messages

Practice Activity

Take a moment to review your email inbox. Are there any suspicious messages that ask you to click on a link? If so, delete them immediately. If you're not sure, reach out to your IT department for assistance.
